I am an alumnus from the [University Lubumbashi](https://uniu.ac.cd), After my master graduation at the [African Institute for Mathematical Sciences Senegal](https://aims-senegal.org), I acted as Teacher Assistant at the [Nouveaux Horizons University](www.unhorizons.org) in DRCongo during from 2017 to 2020. After one year and half as TA, I was promoted as the Academic Secretary of the Faculty of computer sciences and technology, also Coordinator of the exam session of the all faculty many times. Since November 2020, I started his PhD at the faculty of sciences of the [Alexandria University](https://www.alexu.edu.eg/index.php/en/) in Machine Learning. In 2025, I am selected after regular process as Tutor at the [AIMS Cameroon](https://aims-cameroon.org/) in Limbe. My main interests are Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Topological data analysis, Unsupervised machine learning, Data science with application in Health informatics and social subjects.
